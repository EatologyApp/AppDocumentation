Account Deletion Instructions for Eatology

Welcome to the account deletion instructions page for the Eatology app.

Steps to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  • Open the Korean recipes app.
  • Go to your profile settings.
  • Click on the "Delete Account" button.
  • Confirm your action. Your account will be deleted within 30 days.

Data to Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, all your personal data will be deleted, including:

  • Activity history
  • User preferences
  • Favorite recipes and other settings

Some data, such as financial transactions, may be retained as required by law.

Data Retention Period

After requesting account deletion, data will be deleted within 30 days. Some data may be retained for legal reasons (e.g., for accounting or tax purposes) as required by law.

If you have any questions or issues, please contact our support team at Eatologyapp@gmail.com.

Note: If you have not initiated the account deletion process, your data remains secure and is used only for providing services.
